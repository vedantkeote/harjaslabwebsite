India's Best University for Women

Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University (IGDTUW), located at Kashmere gate Delhi, is one of the leading institutions of learning in India.
Since inception in 1968 as an institute for higher education in technology and engineering by the then Member of Parliament,
Shri Gopi Krishnan, it is committed to grooming students for leadership roles in the society.
